# Design Studio

The Design Studio lets hotels customize the look and feel of their booking page. It's available in the **Booking Admin** under the Design Studio section.

## What can be customized

### Hero Image
- The large banner image at the top of the booking page
- Hotels can upload their own image
- Recommended size: 1920x800px or similar wide format

### Colors
- **Primary color** — used for buttons, links, and highlights
- **Secondary color** — used for accents
- Changes are reflected across the entire booking page

### Font Pairing
- Hotels can choose from a selection of font combinations
- Affects headings and body text on the booking page

### Live Preview
- All changes show up in a live preview panel
- Hotels can see exactly how their booking page will look before saving

## How to help a hotel with their design

1. Log in to **[admin.booking.vayada.com](https://admin.booking.vayada.com)** as the hotel
2. Go to **Design Studio** in the sidebar
3. Upload their hero image, set their brand colors, pick fonts
4. Use the live preview to check the result
5. Click **Save**

:::tip
If a hotel sends you their brand guidelines (colors, fonts, logo), you can set this up for them using the Design Studio before they even log in — just use their account or set it up via an invite code.
:::
